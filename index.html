PURPOSE

TutorMatch connects students with expert tutors for personalized, one-on-one learning across a wide range of subjects. Our platform simplifies the search for qualified tutors by matching learners based on their academic needs, learning style, and schedule. Whether tackling challenging coursework, preparing for exams, or mastering new skills, TutorMatch empowers students to achieve their goals through tailored support from vetted educators. By bridging gaps in education accessibility, we foster confidence, academic growth, and lifelong success—anytime, anywhere.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Build a scalable and efficient platform that supports at least 500 concurrent users, maintaining 90% uptime and an average system response time of 2 seconds or less, ensuring smooth performance as usage grows.
  2. Develop a responsive and user-friendly Angular-based session booking interface that renders available tutors within 1 second of selection input and allows students to complete a booking in no more than 3 clicks.
  3. Implement a secure authentication system with JWT-based authentication, ensuring that 99.9% of login requests are processed within 2 seconds, while protecting user data with encryption and role-based access control (RBAC).
  4. Ensure all tutor-student matching requests process within 1.5 seconds under peak load (500 concurrent users). Conduct stress tests, targeting less than 2% error.
